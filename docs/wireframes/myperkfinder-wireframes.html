MyPerkFinder — Product Wireframes

A world-class, scalable deal-discovery platform. This preview covers 30 wireframes across the public shopper experience, PWA retention, admin operations, affiliate imports, and analytics. All content is mock data — no real APIs.

🧭

How to use this preview

Use the left sidebar to jump between screens. Key shopper screens (Homepage, Deals, Deal Detail, Saved, Alerts) show both desktop and app-like mobile layouts. Interactive bits — coupon reveal, tabs, and toggles — are clickable. The URL pill (top-right) shows the intended route for each screen.

🛍️

Public Shopper Experience

Fast, mobile-first browsing of deals, coupons, stores & categories. Strong deal cards with savings, discount badges, verified status and expiry urgency. The Deal Detail page is the conversion hub with a prominent “Go to Deal” CTA and affiliate disclosure placed right beside it.

📲

PWA & Retention

Install banners, saved deals, and a granular alerts center (price-drop, coupon-expiry, daily digest). Push-notification permission states are wireframed as placeholders so future price-drop alerts slot in cleanly without redesign.

🗂️

Admin Review Experience

A production-grade review queue with confidence scores, validation flags, side-by-side raw→normalized comparison, and bulk moderation. Statuses (draft, active, needs_review, expired, rejected, archived) drive the whole offer lifecycle.

🔌

Affiliate Import Operations

Source connections (CJ, Rakuten, Impact, Awin, Walmart, eBay, feeds, merchant emails), import job timelines, raw record inspection, merchant/category/brand mapping, LLM extraction review, and duplicate merge — the operational backbone that keeps offers accurate at scale.

📈

Analytics & Scalability

Clicks over time, top deals/stores/categories, search terms, traffic sources, subscriber & email analytics, and per-deal performance. The component library and consistent tokens keep the design system scalable as new surfaces are added.

♿

Accessibility & Trust

Semantic headings, keyboard-visible focus rings, strong contrast, clear labels and alt-text placeholders. Clear, repeated affiliate disclosures and “verified today” signals build shopper trust throughout.
